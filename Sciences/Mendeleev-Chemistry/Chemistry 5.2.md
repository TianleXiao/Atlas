## How catalyst work in reaction?
  
- Catalysts work by giving the reaction an alternative reaction pathway to reach the products with a lower activation energy.
# States of Catalysts
- **Homogeneous Catalysts**
  - Are in the same state (liquid/solid) as the reactants
  - Mix very well with reactants, have high surface area
- **Heterogeneous catalysts**
  - Are in different state to reactants
  - Easy to separate from the reactants afterwards
