## Decision making tool: the Ansoff matrix
The Ansoff matrix was developed by Igor Ansoff (1918–2002). It examines strategies in terms of the goods and services offered by a business to meet customer needs and wants and the markets a business competes in. It highlights four possible strategies open to businesses, as illustrated hereby in the figure.

---
## 7Ps
+ Product
+ Price
+ Place
+ Promotion
+ People
+ Process
+ Physical Evidence
---
## 4Ps
+ Product
+ Price
+ Place
+ Promotion
