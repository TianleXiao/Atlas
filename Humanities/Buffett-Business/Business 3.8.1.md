## Investment appraisal
The learning outcomes (or assessment objectives) for this section are:
- Investment opportunities using payback period
- Investment opportunities using average rate of return or ARR
- Investment opportunities using net present value or NPV (HL only)
→ Investment appraisal enables decision makers to make a more informed decision, based on quantitative methods. Whilst qualitative factors may also be considered in decision-making, these are rather subjective in nature.
