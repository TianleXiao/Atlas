## Convenience sampling advantages
- Samples are created by using research subjects who are easily and readily accessible.
- It is the quickest, easiest, and cheapest method of sampling.

## Sampling errors
- A sampling error occurs when a researcher inappropriately designs market research. Such errors are caused by poor sample design or poor methodology. For example, a group of students doing research for a school project may find convenience sampling easier than quota sampling. Sampling errors can also be caused by the sample size being too small or having bias built into the research.
- A non-sampling error may happen when mistakes in sampling methodology are not attributed to the researcher's human errors. For example, untruthful answers by respondents distort the results and findings.
