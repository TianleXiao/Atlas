## Market analyses disadvantages
- It can be expensive to use market analyses, as they usually have to be paid for. For example, data on Statista is predominantly only available to its subscribers. Hence, market analyses may not be readily available to smaller organizations. For example, a Euromonitor report on the consumer electronics industry in France (relevant to the November 2020 Ducaul Aspirateurs Paper 1 Case Study) is priced at £1,725 ($2,330!). A ReportLinker market analysis report on the global Esports industry (relevant to the BTEC Business examination in January 2021) is priced at $5,500!
- Market analyses can become outdated rather quickly, unless the source provides regular updates.
- The data is not exclusive to any organization, as rivals can also purchase these reports, or subscribe to agencies that provide up-to-date analyses.
